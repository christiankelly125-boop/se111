Things I Enjoy

Gaming

Video gaming is one of my biggest hobbies. I enjoy a variety of games from sandbox builders like Minecraft and Terraria to action-adventure games like the Legend of Zelda series.

Creative Projects

I love working on creative projects, whether it's game modding, digital art, or exploring new software tools. There's something satisfying about creating something from nothing.

Music

Music is a big part of my life. I enjoy discovering new artists, exploring different genres, and appreciate how music can enhance my gaming and creative experiences.

Movies

I'm a fan of movies across many genres, from action blockbusters to thoughtful dramas. Film is a great way to unwind and experience different stories and perspectives.
